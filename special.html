Help Us Build What's Next

We are part of a Cru team working to strengthen and grow the church in Moldova — specifically among young people. Every year, our team plans projects that create real moments of change in students' lives.

Here is what your gift makes possible:

English Week — Once or twice a year, we bring students together for a full week of English learning, community, and Gospel conversations. For many of them, it is the first time they hear about Christ in a meaningful way.

Extreme Camp — Every summer, we take students into nature for a week of adventure, discipleship, and life-changing experiences. It is one of the highlights of the year for everyone involved.

Ongoing discipleship — After the big events, the real work begins. We walk with students week by week, helping them grow in their faith and become leaders in their churches.
