We are a Cru family serving in Moldova with a calling to plant a church for every 1,000 people in our nation. Behind that vision is a team — people who give their time, energy, and faith to see it happen.

Here is what your gift makes possible:

Church Planting — We work directly with local churches and leaders to start new communities of faith in places where there are none. Every new church is a new center of hope for its neighborhood.

Outreach Events — Throughout the year, we organize events that bring people together — concerts, camps, English clubs — creating natural bridges to Gospel conversations.

Ongoing Discipleship — After first encounters, the real work begins. We walk with people week by week, helping them grow in faith and become the next generation of church leaders in Moldova.

To sustain this work, our team needs $1,700 per month. Every dollar goes directly to the people and projects we serve.
